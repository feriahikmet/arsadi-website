FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

ARSADI – “Towards a Remedy”


Out via Rexius Records September 26 - Waterfall release with “Closer”


Uppsala’s ARSADI return with “Towards a Remedy,” a widescreen slice of Swedish indie rock that marries lyrical introspection with an anthemic, open-road rush. Built on shimmering synths, luminous guitars and a pulsing rhythm section, the track captures a late-night drive up the E4—searching, steady, and ultimately cathartic.


Frontman Anthony Bachtiar and Nils Öberg sketch the scene in vivid detail: violet skies over spruce forests, gravel roads that slow time, and a quiet conviction that resilience is learned, not given. The refrain—“Sometimes down is the way to the top”—lands like a mantra, turning private doubt into a communal sing-along.


Produced by ARSADI alongside Christoffer Parning Finnson, mixed by Finnson and mastered by Jacob Alberts, “Towards a Remedy” channels the band’s signature blend: literate, emotionally honest songwriting framed by textured, arena-leaning sonics. Malkolm Bonander’s drums drive the song’s forward motion while Ida Bachtiar’s harmonies add warmth to the widescreen chorus.


“Towards a Remedy” continues ARSADI’s waterfall rollout following the first single “Closer,” with the next chapter “Neon Moonlight” on the horizon. It’s a statement of intent from a band that balances thoughtfulness with play—the sage’s eye for detail, the crowd’s love for release.


For fans of Deportees, Shout Out Louds, Parcels, Karakou and the melodic expansiveness of modern indie pop/rock, ARSADI offer a generous, big-hearted anthem built for late-night highways and festival fields alike.

ARSADI Bio

Indie Rock with a Grand Pop Heart


ARSADI crafts emotionally rich indie rock shaped by shimmering synths, rhythmic guitar work, and sweeping vocals. Based in Uppsala, Sweden, their sound blends alt-leaning rock with the infectious energy of arena pop—perfect for euphoric playlists and emotionally charged road trip soundtracks. Fans of kent, Deportees, Shout Out Louds, and Coldplay will feel right at home in ARSADI’s lyrically rich world full of high emotion and captivating anthems.


Formed after frontman Anthony Bachtiar returned from music studies in London, ARSADI grew from solo demos into a full band driven by artistic exploration and playful energy. The group—Anthony, Nils Öberg, Feria Hikmet, and Malkolm Bonander—earned early acclaim through groove-tinged, eclectic pop that evolved into a bold, unified sound: Indie rock with soaring melodies, textured production, anthemic pop hooks, and emotional resonance.


Their 2023 EP Introspections marked a turning point, praised for its polished production and lyrical depth. Tracks like “Overcome the Night” and “Fade In Fade Out” explore anxiety, resilience, and the pursuit of happiness through poetic allegories and references to literature and visual art.


After winning regional competitions and repeatedly playing the biggest stage at Uppsala's Kulturnatten Festival, ARSADI enters a new chapter with Rexius Records. A fresh wave of releases will continue channeling their emotionally driven, high-energy sound toward the big stage.
